About Sky Park

Pokémon Sky Park is one of the most popular attractions in the world. With many Pokémon designed rides such as Pikachu roller coaster and Pokémon ball Ferris wheel. All Pokémon lovers and visitors will get the chance to ride our state-of-the-art adrenaline factory and enjoy the spectacular views at the top. Not only that, our visitors can also choose from facilities such as swing boat, roller coaster and Mega King tower. These attractions will definitely give you an unforgettable memory.

Attractions

Roller Coaster Adult

One of the tallest Roller Coaster recorded in Asia full of excitement, exhilaration, fear and pure heaven. The maximum speed of the Roller Coaster is 240 km/h!

[Restrictions] Only for visitors above 18 years old.

Roller Coaster Kids

Time for our little riders to hop on the adorable Pikachu roller coaster for a fun-filled ride. Do not miss out our lucky draw and get a chance to win prizes.

[Weight Restrictions] Maximum: 30kg for Single Rider and 60kg for Double Rider

Mega King Tower

Grab the only chance to reach the skies in our country when you riding this Mega King Tower. A park favourite among all with the highest ride history.

[Restrictions] Only for visitors above 18 years old.

Swing Boat

While most boats sail this one flies. They might be bumpy surprises along the way so come and get in our boat. This is a ride not to be missed by one bring along your children, friends and families and check this out.

[Restrictions] None

Ferris Wheel

We designed this based on the Pokémon ball idea which enables the passenger to experience that they are staying inside the Pokémon ball and turning themself into a Pokémon.

[Remarks] Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.

Flying Sky Tower

Combination of Mega King Tower and Roller Coaster. Visitors can watch the beautiful scenery especially the sunsets on this facility and enjoy the moment of flying in the sky.

[Restrictions] Only for visitors above 18 years old.
